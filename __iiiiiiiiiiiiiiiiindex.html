Wraith

Interdimensional fighter

Rafe is a whirlwind fighter capable of carrying out deadly attacks as well as manipulating space-time, creating rifts in the fabric of reality. But these opportunities cost her dearly. A few years ago, she woke up in the IMC with no memory of her life. Senior Science Pilot Rene Blazy, who volunteered for her own experiments ... who was betrayed and thrown into a cell ... disappeared. There was only a timid girl, frightened by the incessant cacophony of voices in her head, until another copy of her appeared and taught her to listen attentively. Following her example, Rafe found the strength to break out of prison and escape into another reality - this reality.


Finally free, but still devoid of memories, Rafe continues on her way, hoping to find out who she was before the experiments. Many Apex Games are held at old IMC bases, and Rafe battles among the legends to get to these research centers - and the secrets of his past. Now at the Games it has its own, and with each match it comes closer to the truth ...
